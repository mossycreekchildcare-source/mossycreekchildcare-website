Welcome to Mossy Creek Childcare

Mossy Creek Childcare is a nature-inspired early learning center where children thrive through play-based experiences. We believe that nature is the best classroom, offering endless opportunities for exploration, discovery, and growth.

Our experienced educators guide children ages 0 to 12 years through meaningful experiences that develop physical skills, creativity, and a deep connection to the natural world. We offer Infant & Toddler programs (ages 0 to 36 months) in safe, nurturing environments at both our Chilliwack and Hope locations.

Chilliwack Location: 46017 Lewis Ave, Chilliwack BC V2P 3E2 (7:30 AM - 4:30 PM)
Hope Location: 881 Rupert Street, Hope BC V0X 1L0 (8:00 AM - 4:00 PM)
